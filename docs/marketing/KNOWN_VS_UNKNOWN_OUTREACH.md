# KNOWN vs UNKNOWN OUTREACH STRATEGY

**The fundamental difference:** Trust vs. Cold persuasion  
**The goal:** Convert warm leads faster, cold leads systematically

---

## PART 1: WARM OUTREACH (Known Contacts)

### Who They Are
- YC batchmates
- Former colleagues (Wipro, EY, MedPiper)
- Industry acquaintances
- Friends in tech
- LinkedIn 1st connections
- Past clients

### The Advantage
- **Trust already exists**
- **Response rate: 30-50%**
- **Faster to meeting: 1-3 days**
- **Higher conversion: 20-30%**

### The Strategy: Direct Ask

**Don't beat around the bush. They know you. Ask directly.**

---

### TIER 1: Close Network (YC, Close Friends, Former Managers)

**Relationship:** Strong trust, personal connection  
**Approach:** Honest about situation + specific ask

**Template:**
```
Hey [Name],

I'm reaching out because I need your help. After 5.5 years at MedPiper, 
the company is winding down and I'm looking for my next role.

What I'm looking for:
• Senior IC: Staff Engineer, AI Engineer, or Technical PM
• Applied AI, document processing, healthcare SaaS
• ₹70-80L base
• Bengaluru or remote

I have 14 years experience, YC S20 background, and recently built 24 
projects keeping my technical skills current. Available immediately.

Do you know of any openings at your company or in your network? 
Happy to chat briefly if you have time this week.

Thanks,
Pranay
```

**Follow-Up Strategy:**
- Day 3: "Hey, following up on this. Any thoughts?"
- Day 7: "Would love to catch up either way—coffee sometime?"
- Day 14: Share relevant article/insight + soft ask

**Success Metric:** 50%+ will offer help or make intro

---

### TIER 2: Professional Network (Colleagues, Industry Contacts)

**Relationship:** Professional respect, occasional contact  
**Approach:** Value-first + clear ask

**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

Hope you're doing well at [Company]. I saw [company news/product launch]—
congrats!

Quick update: After 5.5 years co-founding MedPiper (YC S20), I'm 
exploring new opportunities. Grew the company to $1M ARR, led 
product/platform, achieved compliance.

Now looking for senior IC roles where I can apply this experience—
applied AI, document processing, technical product management.

If [Company] is hiring or you know of any opportunities in your 
network, would appreciate an intro. Portfolio: [URL]

Happy to catch up either way!

Best,
Pranay
```

**Follow-Up Strategy:**
- Day 5: Share relevant insight/resource
- Day 10: "Following up—any thoughts on who I should talk to?"
- Day 21: Soft check-in

**Success Metric:** 30-40% will respond, 10-20% will make intro

---

### TIER 3: Weak Ties (Met once, LinkedIn connections, acquaintances)

**Relationship:** Vague memory, loose connection  
**Approach:** Remind who you are + value proposition + ask

**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

We [met at X / connected via Y / were introduced by Z]. I'm a YC alum 
with 14 years experience in product and engineering.

Quick update: After 5.5 years building a healthcare startup to $1M ARR, 
I'm exploring senior IC opportunities in applied AI and document 
processing.

Built 24 projects recently exploring voice AI, OCR, RAG systems: [URL]

If you know of any companies hiring for senior technical roles, would 
love your perspective. Targeting ₹70L+ range, available immediately.

Thanks!
Pranay
```

**Follow-Up Strategy:**
- Day 7: Share relevant content/article
- Day 14: Brief follow-up

**Success Metric:** 15-25% response rate

## PART 2: COLD OUTREACH (Unknown Contacts)

### Who They Are
- CTOs at target companies
- Founders of interesting startups
- Hiring managers
- Recruiters
- LinkedIn 2nd/3rd connections

### The Challenge
- **No trust established**
- **Response rate: 5-15%**
- **Longer to meeting: 1-2 weeks**
- **Lower conversion: 2-5%**

### The Strategy: Value + Relevance + Credibility

**You need to earn attention. Every word must work.**

---

### TIER 1: Targeted Cold (Research-based)

**Research Time:** 5-10 min per person  
**Target:** High-value prospects only

**Research Checklist:**
- [ ] Recent company news/funding
- [ ] Their recent posts/articles
- [ ] Mutual connections
- [ ] Tech stack they use
- [ ] Their specific role/challenges

**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

Saw your post about [specific topic]. Your point about [specific detail] 
resonated—I've dealt with similar challenges building document 
processing pipelines at scale.

Quick context: YC alum, 5.5 years building healthcare AI (0→$1M ARR). 
Recently shipped 24 projects exploring voice AI, OCR, RAG: [GitHub]

Not sure if [Company] is hiring, but I'd love to learn about your 
approach to [specific technical challenge]. Worth a brief conversation?

Best,
Pranay
[Portfolio URL]
```

**Why This Works:**
- Shows you did homework
- Demonstrates relevance
- Offers value (insight)
- Low-pressure ask

**Follow-Up Strategy:**
- Day 4: Share relevant resource
- Day 8: Soft bump
- Day 14: Final follow-up

**Success Metric:** 15-25% response rate

---

### TIER 2: Scaled Cold (Volume-based)

**Research Time:** 1-2 min per person  
**Target:** 50-100 outreaches/week

**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

YC alum here (S20) with 14 years product/engineering experience. 
Co-founded healthcare startup, grew to $1M ARR, led platform/AI 
integration.

Recently returned to hands-on work—built 24 projects exploring AI, 
voice, OCR, agents: [Portfolio]

Is [Company] currently hiring senior ICs? I'd love to learn about 
what you're building.

Best,
Pranay
```

**Why This Works:**
- YC credential opens doors
- Metrics show results
- Portfolio provides proof
- Clear ask

**Follow-Up Strategy:**
- Day 3: Bump
- Day 7: Final follow-up

**Success Metric:** 5-10% response rate

---

### TIER 3: Spray & Pray (Low-value)

**Use:** Only in desperation  
**Method:** Generic templates, mass send

**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

I'm a 14-year veteran exploring new opportunities. YC alum, $1M ARR 
track record, recently built 24 AI projects.

Looking for senior IC roles. Available immediately.

Portfolio: [URL]

Thanks,
Pranay
```

**Success Metric:** 2-5% response rate (but volume may compensate)

**Warning:** Can damage reputation if overused. Use sparingly.

## THE CONVERSION FUNNEL

### Known Contacts (Warm)

```
Reach Out (100%)
    ↓ 50% respond
Conversation
    ↓ 60% offer help
Introduction / Referral
    ↓ 30% convert to interview
Interview
    ↓ 50% get offer
Offer Accepted
```

**Math:** 10 warm outreaches → 5 responses → 3 offers to help → 1 intro → 0.5 offers

### Unknown Contacts (Cold)

```
Reach Out (100%)
    ↓ 10% respond
Conversation
    ↓ 20% agree to meeting
Meeting
    ↓ 30% convert to interview
Interview
    ↓ 40% get offer
Offer Accepted
```

**Math:** 100 cold outreaches → 10 responses → 2 meetings → 0.6 interviews → 0.24 offers

**Conclusion:** Warm outreach is 5-10x more efficient. Prioritize known contacts.

## NURTURE STRATEGY (For Non-Immediate Responses)

### The Long Game

Not everyone can help immediately. Stay top of mind.

**Month 1:** Active outreach
**Month 2-3:** Nurture with value
**Month 4+:** Check in quarterly

**Nurture Tactics:**
- Share relevant articles
- Comment on their posts
- Congratulate on milestones
- Offer insights/resources
- Soft check-ins

**Example Nurture Message:**
```
Hi [Name],

Saw [Company] just [milestone]. Congrats!

Reminded me of our conversation about [topic]. I came across this 
article that might be relevant: [link]

Hope all is well!

Pranay
```

## THE REFERRAL AMPLIFICATION

### Turning One Intro Into Many

**When someone offers to help:**

```
"Thanks so much! I'm looking for:
• Companies: [List 5 target companies]
• Roles: Staff Engineer, AI Engineer, Technical PM
• People: CTOs, VPs Engineering, Founders

If you know anyone at these companies or in similar roles, would 
love an intro. Also happy to chat with anyone in your network 
who might have advice!"
```

**The Ask:**
- Specific companies
- Specific roles
- Specific people

**Make it easy for them to help.**

## FINAL RECOMMENDATIONS

### The 80/20 Rule

**Spend 80% of time on warm outreach (known contacts)**  
**Spend 20% of time on cold outreach (unknown)**

**Why:**
- 5-10x higher conversion
- Faster results
- Better fit
- Less rejection fatigue

### The Emergency Exception

**If you have ZERO warm leads:**
- Flip to 80% cold, 20% warm
- Build warm leads from cold (every meeting is future warm lead)
- Join communities (YC, tech meetups) to create warm connections

### The Golden Rule

**"Known contacts get personal messages. Unknown contacts get value-first messages."**

---

**Bottom Line:** Warm outreach is your fast track. Cold outreach is your volume play. Use both, but prioritize warm.
