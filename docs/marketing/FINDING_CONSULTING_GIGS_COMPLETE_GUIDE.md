## CATEGORY 1: PLATFORMS (Active Hunting)

### 1. Upwork

**Strategy:**
- Filter by "US/UK clients only" (higher budgets)
- Filter by "$5K+ spent" (proven payers)
- Filter by "Urgent" or "Less than 5 proposals" (less competition)

**Profile Optimization:**
```
Title: "YC Alum | AI & Automation Expert | 14 Years"

Overview:
"I'm a YC-backed co-founder with 14 years of product and engineering 
experience. I specialize in:

• AI/ML prototypes (document extraction, voice AI, RAG)
• Workflow automation (reduce manual work by 80%+)
• Technical advisory (architecture, AI strategy)
• Fast delivery (2-4 weeks for MVPs)

I don't just write code—I understand business impact. Every project 
starts with 'what problem are we solving?' and ends with working 
software that delivers ROI.

Recent projects:
→ Document extraction system: 45K+ fields, reduced processing by 2 days
→ Voice transcription app: macOS native, Whisper-powered
→ RAG pipeline: 30+ healthcare clients, LLM-powered Q&A

YC S20 alum. $1M ARR track record. Available immediately.

Let's build something."
```

**Proposal Template:**
```
Hi [Client Name],

I built something very similar at my previous startup—document 
processing pipeline handling 45K+ metadata fields with OCR and AI.

Quick questions:
1. What's driving the timeline? (Board meeting, customer deadline?)
2. What does "done" look like? (Specific deliverables)
3. What's the budget range? (So I can scope appropriately)

I can deliver an MVP in 2-3 weeks. Available to start immediately.

Recent work: [GitHub/portfolio link]

Worth a 15-min call to discuss?

Best,
Pranay
```

**Pricing on Upwork:**
- AI Prototype: $8-12K
- Automation: $4-8K
- Advisory: $2-4K
- Hourly: $100-150/hour

**Time Investment:** 1-2 hours/day applying
**Expected ROI:** 2-4 projects/month at $5K average = $10-20K

## PRICING MODELS

### 1. Fixed Fee (Recommended)

**Best for:** Defined scope projects
**Advantage:** Client knows cost upfront
**Risk:** Scope creep

**How to price:**
- Estimate hours × hourly rate × 1.5 (buffer)
- Example: 40 hours × $100 × 1.5 = $6K

---

### 2. Hourly

**Best for:** Ongoing advisory, unclear scope
**Advantage:** Paid for all time
**Risk:** Client may question hours

**Rates:**
- AI/ML engineering: $100-200/hour
- Advisory: $150-250/hour
- Automation: $75-150/hour

---

### 3. Retainer

**Best for:** Ongoing support
**Advantage:** Predictable income
**Risk:** Client may underutilize

**Packages:**
- 10 hours/month: $1,500
- 20 hours/month: $2,800
- 40 hours/month: $5,000

---

### 4. Value-Based

**Best for:** High-impact projects
**Advantage:** Aligns with client success
**Risk:** Hard to define value

**Example:**
- "I'll save you $50K/year in manual work"
- Price: $10K (20% of first-year savings)
