## EMERGENCY FINANCIAL VERSION

### When You NEED the Job (But Can't Show Desperation)

**What to Say:**
> "I'm available immediately and can start within 2 weeks. I'm targeting ₹70L which reflects my 14 years of experience and YC background. I'm also open to discussing a sign-on bonus given my quick availability."

**What NOT to Say:**
- "I really need this job"
- "I'll take whatever you offer"
- "My current situation is..."
- Any hint of financial stress

**The Frame:**
- "Immediate availability" = Premium value, not desperation
- "Sign-on bonus" = Business transaction, not charity
- "Targeting ₹70L" = Confidence in worth, not greed
- "Flexible on title" = Team player, not pushover
