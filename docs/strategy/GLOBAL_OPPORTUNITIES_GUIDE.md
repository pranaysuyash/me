| Role | India (₹) | US Remote ($) | US On-site ($) | Multiplier |

| Staff Engineer | 60-90L | $180-250K | $200-300K | 3-4x |

#### Target Companies (Remote-friendly)

**Tier 1: Big Tech (Hard to get, high pay)**
- Google, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft, Apple
- Netflix, Airbnb, Stripe, Uber
- Requirements: Strong CS fundamentals, system design
- Process: 5-8 interviews, 2-3 months

**Tier 2: High-growth Startups (Good balance)**

- HashiCorp, Datadog, Cloudflare
- Requirements: Startup experience, shipping velocity
- Process: 3-5 interviews, 1-2 months

**Tier 3: YC Companies (Network advantage)**
- Deel, Brex, Faire, Razorpay (US offices)
- Any YC company hiring remotely
- Requirements: YC network, 0-1 experience
- Process: 2-4 interviews, 2-4 weeks

**Tier 4: Remote-first Companies (Easier entry)**
- GitLab, Zapier, Buffer, Automattic
- Doist, Toptal, InVision
- Requirements: Remote work experience, async communication
- Process: 2-3 interviews, 2-3 weeks

#### Interview Prep (US Market)

**System Design (Critical):**
- Design scalable systems
- Trade-off discussions
- Focus: Distributed systems, microservices

**Behavioral (Important):**
- "Tell me about a time..."
- STAR format answers
- Leadership principles (Amazon-style)

**Coding (Standard):**
- LeetCode Medium/Hard
- Focus: Arrays, trees, graphs, DP

**Cultural Fit:**
- Startup mindset
- Ownership mentality
- Communication skills

#### Visa/Tax Considerations

**Employment Models:**

1. **Contractor (1099)**
   - You invoice US company
   - Pay taxes in India
   - No visa needed
   - Higher rate ($100-200/hour)
   - No benefits

2. **Employee through India Entity**
   - Company has India office
   - Standard Indian employment
   - Local salary (lower)
   - Full benefits

3. **Employee through EOR (Employer of Record)**
   - Deel, Remote.com, Papaya Global
   - US company hires through EOR
   - You get Indian employment
   - US-level salary
   - Best of both worlds

**Tax:**
- India tax resident if >182 days in India
- DTAA (Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement) between India-US
- Consult CA for optimization

## PART 3: PRACTICAL CONSIDERATIONS

### Time Zone Management

**US West Coast (PST/PDT):**
- India: 12:30 PM - 9:00 PM IST
- Best overlap: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM IST (morning in US)
- Schedule: Work India morning, US calls evening

**US East Coast (EST/EDT):**
- India: 9:30 PM - 6:00 AM IST
- Best overlap: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM IST
- Schedule: Late evening calls OR async-first

**Europe (CET):**
- India: 3:30 PM - 12:00 AM IST
- Best overlap: 3:30 PM - 6:00 PM IST
- Schedule: Afternoon calls work well

**Singapore:**
- India: 9:30 AM - 6:00 PM IST
- Best overlap: 9:30 AM - 3:00 PM IST
- Schedule: Normal India working hours

**Dubai:**
- India: 10:30 AM - 7:00 PM IST
- Best overlap: 10:30 AM - 4:00 PM IST
- Schedule: Normal India working hours

### Payment & Invoicing

**For US Clients:**

**Option 1: Wire Transfer**
- Pros: Direct, lower fees
- Cons: Takes 2-3 days
- Best for: $5K+ payments

**Option 2: Wise (formerly TransferWise)**
- Pros: Fast (1-2 days), low fees (0.5-1%)
- Cons: Need client to use Wise
- Best for: Recurring payments

**Option 3: PayPal**
- Pros: Easy, instant
- Cons: High fees (4-5%), FX markup
- Best for: Small payments (<$2K)

**Option 4: Deel/Remote.com**
- Pros: Handles contracts, taxes, compliance
- Cons: 3-5% fees
- Best for: Long-term engagements

**For EU Clients:**

**Option 1: SEPA Transfer**
- Pros: Low cost, fast (1 day)
- Cons: Need EUR account
- Best for: Regular EU clients

**Option 2: Wise**
- Same as above

**For Singapore/Dubai:**

**Option 1: Wire Transfer**
- Standard international wire
- 1-2 days
- Reasonable fees

**Invoicing Tools:**
- FreshBooks (good for international)
- Wave (free, basic)
- Xero (comprehensive)
- Simple: Google Docs + manual tracking

### Contracts & Legal

**For International Clients:**

**Jurisdiction:**
- Specify: "Governed by laws of [State/Country]"
- Prefer: Delaware (US) or UK law (well-established)
- Avoid: Complex local jurisdictions

**Dispute Resolution:**
- Specify: Arbitration in [neutral location]
- Alternative: Online dispute resolution

**IP Ownership:**
- Standard: Client owns all work product
- Exception: You retain right to use techniques/methods

**Liability:**
- Cap liability at fees paid
- Exclude indirect damages

**Force Majeure:**
- Include pandemic, natural disasters, etc.

**Template:** Use LawDepot, Rocket Lawyer, or lawyer-drafted

### Tax Considerations

**India Tax Resident:**
- If >182 days in India = Indian tax resident
- Tax on global income
- File ITR annually
- Pay advance tax quarterly

**DTAA (Double Taxation Avoidance):**
- India has DTAAs with US, UK, most of EU
- Prevents double taxation
- Claim foreign tax credit
- Consult CA for optimization

**GST:**
- Export of services = 0% GST
- LUT (Letter of Undertaking) filing required
- Get GST registration if not already

**Professional CA Recommended:**
- International tax is complex
- Worth ₹10-20K for consultation
- Can save lakhs in taxes

## PART 4: VISA PATHWAYS (If Relocation Desired)

### US Visa Options

**H-1B (Employment Visa):**
- Requires: Employer sponsorship
- Lottery system (30% acceptance)
- Valid: 3 years, renewable to 6
- Path to: Green card

**L-1 (Intra-company Transfer):**
- Requires: Work at India office 1+ year
- Transfer to US office
- No lottery
- Valid: 3-5 years

**O-1 (Extraordinary Ability):**
- Requires: Demonstrated extraordinary ability
- YC + $1M ARR + speaking/writing could qualify
- No lottery
- Valid: 3 years, renewable

**TN (Canada/Mexico citizens only):**
- Not applicable for Indians

**EB-1/EB-2 (Green Card):**
- Long process (2-5 years)
- Requires: Exceptional ability or advanced degree
- YC + $1M ARR helps case

### UK Visa Options

**Skilled Worker Visa:**
- Requires: Job offer, minimum salary (£26,200)
- Employer sponsors
- Valid: 5 years
- Path to: ILR (Indefinite Leave to Remain)

**Global Talent Visa:**
- Requires: Endorsement in digital technology
- YC + $1M ARR likely qualifies
- No job offer needed
- Valid: 5 years

### EU Blue Card (Germany, etc.)

**Requirements:**
- Job offer
- Minimum salary (€43,800 for shortage occupations)
- University degree OR 5 years experience
- Tech qualifies as shortage occupation

**Advantages:**
- Fast track to permanent residence
- Can move between EU countries
- Family can join immediately

### Singapore EP (Employment Pass)

**Requirements:**
- Job offer
- Minimum salary (S$5,000 for experienced)
- Employer sponsors
- Valid: 2 years, renewable

**Advantages:**
- Close to India
- Easy to get PR after 2 years
- English-speaking

### Dubai Employment Visa

**Requirements:**
- Job offer
- Employer sponsors
- Valid: 2-3 years, renewable

**Advantages:**
- 0% income tax
- Easy process
- Close to India
