## THE CONVERSATIONS

### Conversation 1: The Vent Session

**When:** He needs to let off steam  
**My Role:** Listen, validate, don't fix

**Him:** "My co-founders are assholes. They stopped talking to me. I built this company and now I'm being pushed out."

**Me:** "That sucks, man. That's really unfair. You gave 5.5 years to this. I'd be angry too."

**Me:** "You didn't waste it. You built something. You learned. You grew. Yeah, the ending sucks, but the experience is valuable."

**Him:** "I have a kid. I have debt. What if I don't find a job?"

**Me:** "You will. You have 14 years of experience. YC alum. $1M ARR track record. You're employable. It might take 30-60 days, but you'll land something."

**Action:** Let him vent. Don't try to solve. Just listen.

### Conversation 2: The Action Plan

**When:** He's ready to move forward  
**My Role:** Push, strategize, hold accountable

**Me:** "Alright, enough venting. Let's make a plan. What's the goal?"

**Him:** "Get a job. Pay bills. Survive."

**Me:** "Specifics. Target salary? Timeline?"

**Him:** "₹70L. Within 60 days."

**Me:** "Good. Now the plan. Week 1: 50 applications. Week 2: 50 more. Week 3-4: interviews. I'll review your resume today. I'll make 3 intros this week. I'll be your reference."

**Him:** "That's a lot."

**Action:** Create concrete plan, set deadlines, hold him accountable.

### Conversation 3: The Salary Discussion

**When:** He's lowballing himself

**Him:** "Maybe I should ask for ₹50L. Just to get something."

**Me:** "Hell no. You're worth ₹70-80L minimum. 14 years + YC + $1M ARR. Don't undersell yourself."

**Him:** "But what if I don't get offers?"

**Me:** "Then you negotiate. But start at ₹70L. You're not desperate—you're experienced. There's a difference."

**Him:** "I'm kind of desperate though."

**Me:** "Internally, sure. Externally, confident. They don't need to know your financial stress. They need to know your value."

**Action:** Push him to ask for what he's worth.
